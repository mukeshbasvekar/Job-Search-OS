# Cold Email Sequence

> **How to use:** Fill in your [YOUR_NAME] and [YOUR_INTRO_LINE] placeholders below.
> Use `email_prompt.md` to have AI generate the personalized bullets (Email 1 only).
> Email 2 and 3 are fully automatic — set them up as scheduled follow-ups in Apollo, Instantly, or Lemlist.

---

## Email 1 — First Outreach *(manual — 3 things to fill in per send)*

**Subject:** [Company] + [YOUR_NAME] — [Role Title]

Hi [First Name],

[ONE SPECIFIC SENTENCE about their product, a recent launch, or what genuinely caught your attention. Shows 5 minutes of research. Never generic.]

[YOUR_INTRO_LINE — write this once and never change it. Format: "I [built/led] [Product/Company] — [one-line description] — reached [traction], and before that was [Role] at [Company] ([domain]) where I [top achievement]. Here's why I think I'd be useful at [Company]:"]

- [Bullet 1 — maps to JD Priority A, one bold metric, ~15 words]
- [Bullet 2 — maps to JD Priority B, one bold metric, ~15 words]
- [Bullet 3 — maps to JD Priority C, one bold metric, ~15 words]

Would love a quick 15-min chat if there's interest. If you're not the right person, happy to be pointed to whoever is.

Resume attached.

[YOUR_NAME]
[LinkedIn URL] | [Portfolio URL]

---

### What to Fill In Per Send (3 Things)

1. `[First Name]` — contact's first name
2. `[One specific thing]` — one specific sentence about their product, a recent launch, or something you genuinely noticed. Never generic.
3. `[Bullet 1–3]` — tailor to the role. Lead with metrics and outcomes. Use `email_prompt.md` to generate these.

**The rule:** Never open with your degree or graduation date. Your work/product identity is the hook.

**For very early-stage / founder emails (shorter version):**
Skip the bullet list. Replace with: "I [brief relevant summary]. Happy to share more if useful."

## Email 2 — First Follow-up *(fully automatic, Day 4–5, no edits)*

**Subject:** Re: [Company] + [YOUR_NAME] — [Role Title]

Hi [First Name],

Just following up in case my last note got buried.

Even a quick yes, no, or redirection would mean a lot.

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]

---

## Email 3 — Final Follow-up *(fully automatic, Day 9–10, no edits)*

**Subject:** Re: [Company] + [YOUR_NAME] — [Role Title]

Hi [First Name],

This is my last follow-up — I totally get it if the timing isn't right. If there's ever a fit down the road, or if you could point me to the right person, I'd really appreciate it.

Thank you for your time.

Best,
[YOUR_NAME]

## Sequence Summary

| Email | When | Effort | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Manual — 3 things to fill in | Personalized pitch + redirect ask |
| Email 2 | Day 4–5 | Automatic | Pure bump, zero pressure |
| Email 3 | Day 9–10 | Automatic | Final ask — yes/no/redirect |

> Set Email 2 and 3 as automated follow-ups in your outreach tool (Apollo, Instantly, Lemlist, etc.) triggered only if there is no reply.

## How to Write Role-Specific Bullets

Same rigor as the resume prompt:

1. **JD intake:** List the role's top 3–5 priorities from the JD
2. **Map each bullet to one priority:** Bullet 1 = JD priority A, Bullet 2 = B, Bullet 3 = C
3. **Apply rules:** One idea per bullet, one bold number, ~15 words, curiosity over completeness, peer tone
4. **No generic bullets:** If it could fit any company, rewrite it
