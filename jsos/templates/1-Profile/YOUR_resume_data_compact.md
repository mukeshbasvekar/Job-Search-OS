## Spacing Presets

| Preset | When to Use |
|---|---|
| Fill | Light content (few bullets, large gaps) |
| Standard | Typical 3 experience entries + 1 product |
| Dense | Heavy content (many entries, tight fit) |

Spacing values to adjust in LaTeX:
- `\titlespacing*`: controls space above/below section headers
- `itemsep` / `topsep`: controls space between bullets
- `\expEntry` / `\eduEntry` `vspace`: controls space above each entry
